Alappuzha

Traditional wooden boats converted into floating cottages with bedrooms, dining areas, and sundecks. Perfect for overnight stays.

Athirappilly Waterfalls

It’s a breathtaking sight, especially when the water gushes down in full force, creating misty sprays and rainbows.

Munnar

Munnar is carpeted with endless tea estates, where rolling green hills create postcard-perfect scenery. Visitors can tour tea factories and taste freshly brewed varieties.
