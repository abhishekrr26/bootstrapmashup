Ziro Valley

A picturesque valley surrounded by pine-covered hills and rice fields. It’s home to the Apatani tribe and famous for the Ziro Music Festival, blending culture with natural beauty

Diu Island

A small coastal town with Portuguese heritage, quiet beaches, and historic forts. Unlike Goa, it’s less crowded, offering a peaceful seaside escape.

Lonar Lake

A unique crater lake formed by a meteor impact over 50,000 years ago. Surrounded by forests and ancient temples, it’s a geological wonder that blends history, science, and spirituality
